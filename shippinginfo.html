Shipping & Delivery Policy

Shipping & Delivery

Our domestic orders are shipped using DTDC. You will receive a tracking ID via email, SMS, or WhatsApp from our delivery partner or directly from us.

📦 Delivery Timeline

Standard Orders: 10-15 Business days

Custom Orders: 25 Business days

🚚 Free Shipping Offer

Shipping is FREE for all prepaid orders above ₹500. For orders below this amount, shipping charges will be calculated at the time of checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

❌ No Returns or Exchanges

We do not accept returns or exchanges for our handmade items.

Damaged or Wrong Products

If you received a damaged or wrong product, kindly reach out to us via email at threadsofhopebytj@gmail.com within 72 hours of receiving the order.

Please include the following in your email:

  • Order number
  • Clear photos of the damaged/incorrect item
  • Description of the issue

Missing Packages

If your order has been lost in transit or your tracking shows it was delivered but you did not receive it, please email us at threadsofhopebytj@gmail.com.

📋 Important Information

After an order has been delivered to the requested shipping address, we cannot be held responsible for lost or stolen packages.
