Bring a touch of handmade charm to your hairstyle with our Tulip Scrunchie. Carefully crafted with soft yarn, this scrunchie features delicate tulip-shaped blooms in vibrant pink with lush green leaves, all set against a beautifully wrapped base.

Perfect for adding a pop of color and elegance to ponytails, buns, or simply worn on the wrist as an accessory.

Care Instructions:

• Hand wash gently in cold water with mild detergent.

• Do not wring or twist; gently squeeze out excess water.

• Lay flat on a clean towel and reshape to dry naturally.

• Avoid direct sunlight for long periods to preserve colors.

• Keep away from sharp objects to prevent snagging.
