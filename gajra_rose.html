Care Instructions:

• Hand wash gently in cold water with mild detergent.

• Do not wring or twist; gently squeeze out excess water.

• Lay flat on a clean towel and reshape to dry naturally.

• Avoid direct sunlight for long periods to preserve colors.

• Keep away from sharp objects to prevent snagging.
